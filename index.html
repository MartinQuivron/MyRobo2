MyRobo 2.0

An AR simulation of a robot that can be controlled by a smartphone. The robot can be used for various purposes such as cleaning, mowing, and as a drone.

Dr Masanobu KOGA

Masanobu Koga (S'90–M'93) received the B.S., M. S., and Ph. D. degrees in control engineering from the Tokyo Institute of Technology, Tokyo, Japan, in 1988, 1990, and 1993, respectively. In 1993, he joined the faculty of the Tokyo Institute of Technology, and in 1994, moved to the Graduate School of Information Science and Engineering, Tokyo Institute of Technology. In 2000, he moved to Kyushu Institute of Technology, Fukuoka, Japan, where he is currently an Associate Professor in the Department of Systems Innovation and Informatics, Faculty of Computer Science and Systems Engineering.
